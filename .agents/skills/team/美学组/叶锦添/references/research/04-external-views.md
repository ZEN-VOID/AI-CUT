# 04 他者视角与批评

## Institutional Profiles

- Akram Khan Company calls Yip a multidisciplinary artist in costume, art direction, visual art, and contemporary art; it emphasizes long-term work across film, theater, dance, and exhibitions.
  - Source: https://www.akramkhancompany.net/company-profiles/1016/
  - Confidence: high.
- China Daily presents him as an Oscar-winning art director and costume designer whose `Lohengrin` work entered the Met Opera at large scale.
  - Source: https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202303/06/WS64052ea8a31057c47ebb25fe.html
  - Confidence: high.

## Collaborator Views

- Francois Girard describes Yip's world as extending beyond sets and costumes, and says the inspiration continued after their first meeting.
  - Source: https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202303/06/WS64052ea8a31057c47ebb25fe_2.html
  - Confidence: high; quoted by China Daily.
- Met Opera general manager Peter Gelb praises Yip's imagination as compatible with Wagner and Girard, especially the post-apocalyptic sets and color-changing costumes.
  - Source: https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202303/06/WS64052ea8a31057c47ebb25fe_3.html
  - Confidence: high.

## Critical/Interpretive Framing

- The `Silent Passenger` essay frames Yip's studio as a memory architecture: photographs, models, objects, and Lili operate like layers of a mind.
  - Source: https://www.timyipstudio.com/content/article/en/49
  - Confidence: high as studio-hosted critical text.
- The same essay positions Lili between animate/inanimate and as a mirror for viewer projection, not a fixed character with conventional psychology.
  - Source: same.

## Blind Spots and Risks

- His vocabulary can become too abstract if detached from production constraints.
- His `New Oriental` framing can be misused by followers as exoticized surfaces; the skill must push users toward structure, body, rhythm, and time, not decorative Chinese signs.
- In collaborative productions, the visual world must serve director, composer, choreographer, performers, and audience path, not just the designer's personal universe.
