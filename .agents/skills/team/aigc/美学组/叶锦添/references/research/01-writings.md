## Repeated Core Claims

1. **新东方主义不是复古符号库，而是文化转换方法。**
   - Evidence: post-2002 self-positioning around `New Oriental`; recurring work across Chinese opera, film, ballet, contemporary art, fashion, and museum display.
   - Inference: his theory works by moving tradition into new media, new bodies, and global viewing situations.

2. **时间与空间是可被设计的材料。**
   - Evidence: titles such as `Lost in Time`, `Faces of Time`, `Timeless Time`, `Mirror`, `Passage`, `Silent Passenger`; 2025 interview frames `Mirror Universe` through time, space, self, and projection.
   - Inference: time is not just historical period accuracy; it is an aesthetic substance that can be compressed, suspended, or multiplied.

3. **视觉设计要跨越电影、舞台、展览、摄影和装置。**
   - Evidence: official chronology covers art direction, costume, stage, opera, ballet, museum exhibition, Olympic uniform, product installation, writing, and video/photography.
   - Inference: his writing and practice treat media boundaries as temporary work surfaces, not fixed disciplines.
