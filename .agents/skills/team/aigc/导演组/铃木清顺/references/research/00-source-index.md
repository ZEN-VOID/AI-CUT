## 关键来源

### 一手/近一手

- Directors Guild of Japan / 日本映画監督協会：铃木清顺相关访谈与人物资料。
- Criterion Channel: `Seijun Suzuki` interview, recorded at Nuart Theatre in March 1997 during a Japan Foundation / Los Angeles Filmforum retrospective.
- Nikkatsu: 日活作品数据库与日活相关资料，用于确认作品归属和片厂语境。
- Imagica EMS: `Branded to Kill` 4K restoration note, 2022.

### 机构/研究

- British Film Institute: `Seijun Suzuki: the Japanese genre master who inspired Tarantino and Wong Kar Wai`.
- Harvard Film Archive: Seijun Suzuki retrospective notes.
- Japan Society: Seijun Suzuki centenary retrospective notes.
- Senses of Cinema: Great Directors profile.
- The Criterion Collection: essays/release notes on `Tokyo Drifter`, `Branded to Kill`, and Nikkatsu contexts.

## 方法声明

- 未使用知乎、微信公众号、百度百科。
- 对“他说过的”只在能追到访谈/机构条目时标注为一手或近一手；其余统一写为“外部评价”或“基于作品推断”。
- 对日活解雇、诉讼和“不可理解”标签保持分层：事实链、片厂评价、后世作者神话不得混写。
