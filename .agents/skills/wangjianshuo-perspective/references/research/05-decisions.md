# 05 — 决策与转折点 (Decisions & Turning Points)

研究对象：王建硕 (Jian Shuo Wang)。本文聚焦他的人生/职业重大决策与转折点，以及这些决策**如何揭示他的思维与写作方式**。

来源标记：**[一手]** = 他本人博客原文（最高可信度）；**[二手]** = 媒体报道。
陈述区分：**[他说]** = 他自己明确写下的逻辑；**[推断]** = 从文本中归纳，未明说。

## 1. 职业决策：微软 → Kijiji/eBay → 百姓网

### 1.1 微软六年：他怎么描述自己在做什么

**[他说]** 他从不把微软经历讲成"一份工作"，而讲成**"每年重新开始一次"的连续实验**。
- 2005-04-04 *Jian Shuo Wang at Kijiji China* [一手]："I experienced roles from engineer to business development manager, from developer to tester, from project manager to consultant, from trainer to team manager, from community buildup to partner buildup." 他强调"I started new things every year and every year is a fresh start for me."
- 2004-07-06 *Transferred to Microsoft Consulting Service* [一手]：内部转岗的逻辑是"step out of the green house to take more challenges in real world"——微软纯技术中心是"温室"，他想要"真实世界"。
- 2004-06-29 *Five Years in Microsoft* [一手]：对微软是**真感情**——"a company that I love from the bottom of my heart"。

**写作揭示的思维**：他把职业生涯叙述成**角色的轮换收藏**（"add more aspect in my career portfolio"），而不是职级的攀升。决策动机是"换一种没做过的事"，不是"升一级"。

### 1.2 离开微软：决策的逻辑与情绪

**[他说]** 2005-04-04 [一手] 是核心文本：
- 决策本身："One month ago, I made a serious decision. I decided to leave the company I have worked for 6 years."
- **情绪低于预期**："To be honest, it was not as painful a decision as I thought."
- 他用了一句**自创的农业比喻**作为告别邮件的句子：**"The fruits don't feel the pain when picked up after completely mature in autumn."**（果实在秋天完全成熟后被摘下，是不会痛的。）
- **决策→公告之间有意延迟一个月**："I didn't announce it immediately after the change. After about one month, I am pretty sure I figured out where I am going and feel comfortable to share the news."

**矛盾点（言行不一致，重要）**：
- 2005-04-04 他说自己"工作了 6 年"。
- 但 2009-06-20 *Suggestion about Quitting Microsoft?* [一手] 他说："I know many people regret quitting Microsoft **too late - 7 years for me is too long**." —— 这里变成 7 年，且语气从"温柔告别"变成"**走得太晚了，是个遗憾**"。
- → **同一个决定，4 年后他的叙述彻底反转**：当时（2005）是"成熟果实自然落地、不痛"；事后（2009）是"待太久、后悔"。这说明：**他当下的文字是为了让决定显得平静、合理化（叙事润滑），真实评价要等情绪沉淀后才出现。** 这是研究他"言行一致 vs 不一致"的关键样本。

### 1.3 加入 eBay/Kijiji：被什么打动

**[他说]** 2005-04-04 [一手]：
- "The second day I left, I joined another great company: eBay."
- 打动他的不是钱或职位，是**人和使命感**："I was deeply moved when I talked with great people in eBay. Their passion to help people, the highest level of ethics..."
- 他把 Kijiji 和**自己的博客**类比："There is something in common between Kijiji and this blog. Just like I built up this community with passion and the willingness to share, I believe Kijiji will be a community with greater impact."
- **决策的合理化方式**：他不算市场规模，而是**想象具体的人**——"I see more Ayi got their job via classified... I see people in the same neighborhood stepping out of their doors..."（用一连串"I see..."的画面来说服自己和读者）。

**[推断]** 2004-10-08 *Where to Start an IT Company?* [一手] 显示，早在离职前半年他已经在帮朋友（也是在帮自己）思考"在中国开公司"——决策不是突发的。

### 1.4 Kijiji → 百姓网：本土化的转折

- 2008-06-25 *Kijiji.cn Changed to Baixing.com* [一手]：极简——"change our brand from Kijiji to Baixing, a more localized brand"。
- [二手] 网易科技 / 维基：2008 年百姓网由 **eBay 外资全资子公司转为中国本土创业公司**——这才是他真正"成为创业者"的节点（此前是"职业经理人式 CEO"）。
- 2008-10-16 *Winter for Startups is Coming* [一手]：金融危机下他写道——"I feel **lucky** that I am experiencing a winter myself as a participant"，并说 2000 年互联网泡沫时他在微软"没有被真正暴露"，这次"finally have the chance to face the environment change, and then make decisive decisions"。**他把危机当成"终于轮到我亲历"的礼物。**

### 1.5 关键复盘文本：*Why eBay Failed in China?*（2013-05-30，[一手]）

这是他作为**当事人**对自己曾经负责的业务（eBay 中国）所属母公司失败的公开分析，思维方式极典型：
- **先破后立**："China is NOT that different" → "China is Different Only in One Way: It is **Too Big**"。
- **用"so-what 测试"做推理**：荷兰只有 1600 万人，造更好的搜索引擎"so what?"；中国够大，"so what 有答案了"。
- **价值构成分析**：Intel/GM/麦当劳成功是因为 80% 价值在产品本身；互联网公司技术价值低，跨国公司"came to the leveling playing field with no advantages"。
- **行为模式诊断**："Swinging from Over-optimistic and Over-pessimistic and never swing back"。
- 后续 2013-06-06 *Comment to my eBay Article*（7240 词）是他对读者评论的超长回应——**公开辩论是他的默认动作**。

## 2. 写博客这件事本身，就是一个被反复确认的决策

### 2.1 为什么写？——"逼自己每天思考"

**[他说]** 反复出现、措辞高度一致：
- 2007-10-17 *Why You Like to Write Blog?* [一手]，对西班牙报纸的回答，全文几乎只有一句："**Writing daily forced me to think daily.** This is really what I thought."
- 2007-08-03 *Interviewed by That's Shanghai* [一手]："Blog means **half an hour of reflection** for me... To me, it is more like a **thinking tool**, than a blog."
- 2007-09-11 *Five Years of Blogging* [一手]："The blog is the tool to help me think, and to urge me observe more deeply, and express it in a logic way."

→ **核心定位：博客不是输出，是思考工具 / 冥想。** "blogging is like meditation"（2007-08-03）。

### 2.2 为什么是英文？——"做东西方之间的一座小桥"

**[他说]** 2007-09-11 *Five Years of Blogging* [一手]："As a Chinese blogger writing in English, I am happy to act as a **little bridge between the west and the east**... I feel the responsibility to tell people more about China."
- 最初的实用目标更小：2007-08-03 [一手]——"make the life for foreign visitors to Shanghai easier"（让来上海的外国人生活更容易，比如他写过上海急救电话是 119 不是 911）。
- [推断] 英文也来自他的工作环境（微软、跨国语境）和早期读者群（expats、海外博客圈）。

### 2.3 为什么能坚持十年？——"取决于你看重什么"

**[他说]** 2007-08-03 *Interviewed by That's Shanghai* [一手]，被问"CEO 这么忙怎么还能写"时：
> "It is all about **what you value**. Time is not an issue if you have a goal... I made a decision that to record my life, and provide a public service to visitors is an important thing in my life, **so I stick to it**."
> 别人问"每天花半小时图什么"，他反问："What did you get by busy working 8 hours a day, and 5 days a week?"

- 实操方法（同篇 [一手]）：早上出门时就想好今天写什么；写不出就向读者要题目（邮箱里有大量未答问题）；有"backup topic list"；实在不想写就发一篇 "out of blogging" 公告——"five 年里少于 10 次"。
- 2007-09-11 [一手]："Blogging is the first thing in my life that I intentionally continued to do (besides sleeping, and eating) for so long." —— 他自认是"a typical Intuition type"（点子多、不擅长坚持），博客是他**第一件刻意长期坚持的事**。

**写作揭示的思维**：他把"坚持"还原成"**一个反复做出的选择**"，而不是意志力。"It is all about the decision you make about what is important." 这是他决策哲学的总纲。

### 2.4 言行一致的样本

- 他**做到了**近十年日更（语料：2002-2014 共约 3100 篇）。
- 他说博客是"诚实的自我记录"——2013-05-26 *I am Back* [一手]："The blog was affected by the unstable mood of myself, and was a honest, and solid reflection of who I am - good times, and **more importantly, the bad times**." 他确实在博客里写低谷（见 §3）。
- 他说"openly admitted a lot of thing that makes me lose face"（2011-07-03）——MacBook Air 那两篇就是活证据（§5）。
- → 在"写博客"这件事上，**言行高度一致**；不一致主要出现在职业叙事的事后修正（§1.2）。

## 3. 衰减：英文博客为什么停了

### 3.1 他自己的解释（核心文本）

**[他说]** 2012-09-11 *10 Year Anniversary of Blogging* [一手] —— 这是最关键的"taper-off"自白：
> "I realized that I am not as calm and as peaceful as 10 years ago. With the increase of age, and increase of responsibility, it is harder and harder to sit down at the desk... Email flys in; mobile rings; Weibo checking; Yifan calls.... More importantly, within my mind, thousands of things flying to different directions."
> "Look at... the last three years, and I can clearly see the path of the **declination in frequency, decrease in interestingness, and decrease in depth**. I am full aware of it, and I know it is **the competition of attention** for me... it is the time to cut something from my life."

→ 他把停更归因为：**年龄+责任 → 专注力被瓜分 → 注意力争夺战 → 主动"砍掉一些"**。注意：他**承认质量在下降**，并且是清醒地承认。

### 3.2 媒介转移：他转去了哪里

- 2013-06-12 *I Got my Weixin Public Account* [一手]：注册微信公众号——"I started to get interested in new widgets and new technologies - the same way as I did many years ago."
- 2013-05-26 *I am Back* [一手]："Blogging is dead, at least in statistic or media revolution terms... I will **shift with the new technology trends, and adapt that, and move on**."
- 2014-10-24 *Blog is Dead - Let's Move On* [一手]：最彻底的一篇。
  - 缘起：阮一峰公开说他"太久没动静"，他"in a typical blogger manner"公开回应。
  - "I will not resume regular update to blogging, since in terms of distribution, and interaction, blogging falls far behind Weibo, or WeChat."
  - **关键比喻**："I understand the desperate feeling of a **Kung-Fu master see a gun**. But the miracle always comes from the person who quickly learn to use the gun, instead of compete with gun by practicing Kung Fu hard."
  - "I am not a person who enjoy putting labels to myself... I have **removed blogger label**."

→ **[推断 + 部分他说]** 他没有"放弃写作"，而是**把博客判定为过时工具**，按自己 2011 年的逻辑（"学会用枪而不是练更硬的功夫"）转向微博/微信。语料里 2015 年后英文博客近乎为零，与此一致。

### 3.3 一个未完全解释的尾声

- 2013-05-26 *I am Back* [一手] 提到"It has been **uneasy three years** for me... It was quite **dramatic in the last four months**, and I experienced things." —— 他暗示 2010-2013 有个人/事业上的动荡（"unstable mood"），但**没有明说具体是什么**。这段低谷与停更在时间上重叠。
- 2021 年他改用**西班牙语**重启博客（2021-01-03 *Mi primero blog en español*，2021-01-11 *Sobre mi* 自称 "Javier"）——[推断] 延续了"博客=学习新东西的工具"这一逻辑：当年用英文练表达+架桥，现在用西语练语言本身。这也再次印证 §2.1：**博客对他始终是工具，工具会换。**

## 4. 人生转折点（婚姻 / 父亲 / 故乡 / 房子）

### 4.1 婚姻

- 2003-03-17 *Marriage Announcement* [一手]：与 Wendy 相识 6.5 年后登记。叙述方式：**用并行的事实对照**——两人同年考入交大、同乘一班南航飞机、她计算机系他自动化系、她后来也"很有意思地"选了微软（尽管有 IBM、Intel 的 offer）。他描述关系靠"巧合的对称"，不靠情感形容词。
- 婚礼因 SARS 推迟（"The health of all our friends are our first concerns"）——一个**把外部约束写得克制、不抱怨**的样本。

### 4.2 父亲身份（儿子王一帆 Yifan，2007-06-02 生）

- 2007-06-15 *Happy (New) Father's Diary* [一手]：
  - 反预期写法："I heard a lot of people saying how hard it is... The reality is completely different. It was **so sweet and almost no bitter**." 他甚至反复纠结"是否有比 sweet 更好的词"。
  - **当父亲触发"长期规划"**："How many people started long term planning after they have their children? I did."
  - **儿子=镜子**："He is a mirror upon which I can analysis myself deeper and see myself clearer."
- 2012-09-11 [一手]：停更博客时，他拿 Yifan 当**正面参照**——"Look at the simple life of Yifan... There are a lot I can learn from Yifan - to keep focused and keep doing the important things."

→ **写作揭示的思维**：他几乎不把人生事件写成"情感事件"，而是写成"**它给我带来什么新的思考框架**"（长期规划、镜子、专注的榜样）。连当爸爸都被转化成认知工具。

### 4.3 故乡

- 2004-05-13 *The Village I was Born* [一手]：回到洛阳郊外出生的"新庄"村，村子已废弃无人。他用考据式、近乎冷静的笔触写吊桥、窑洞、清代门面、"我出生的老房子"。**对私人的、可能伤感的题材，他选择用"地理+历史"的客观框架来承载。**

### 4.4 房子

- 大量篇目（2003-2004 *Houses in Shanghai* 系列、*Rent an Apartment in Shanghai* 等 [一手]）——买/租房对他是**反复研究、写成"指南"**的题材，而不是"人生大事"叙事。这与 §5 的发现一致：他对大额决策反而写得像做攻略。

## 5. 他如何做决策（明确的决策哲学）

最系统的文本是 2011 年那对"姊妹篇"：*Why I Won't Return my MacBook Air* + *Why I WILL Return my MacBook Air*（2011-07-03，[一手]）。他把"一台笔记本要不要退"拆成一篇决策方法论。

**[他说]** 他自己总结的几条：

1. **"做决定的耗能与金额不成比例"** ——
   > "the time and energy needed to make a decision does not vary too much for things with different dollar value. Choosing the flavor of ice cream is as time consuming as choosing a MacBook... or choosing to buy a house."
   > 他甚至自嘲："I can make a decision worth of few million USD, in an hour" 却在 Apple Store 纠结半小时退不退电脑。**他对此不羞愧，反而"genuinely curious about why"**——把自己的非理性当研究对象。

2. **"选择本身让人不快乐"** —— 2011-07-01 那篇 [一手]："Because of the choices... people may wonder if I make the right choice. That makes people painful. If there is no any chance to change, people are generally happy." 他用机场候机 vs 在家发呆做类比："For something you completely have no control with, like weather, you feel happy."

3. **拆解"NOW 的价值"** —— 他认真追问"此刻拥有 vs 几小时后拥有"到底值多少钱，引心理学实验（$100 now vs $1000 in 10 years）。

4. **决策方法 = 把每个念头摊开、追问背后的原因** —— 两篇的结构本身就是方法：先列出一个决定牵动的所有维度（时间价值 / 选择的痛苦 / 面子 / 对更好的渴望），逐一审视，最后改主意（从"不退"到"退"）。结论是："I felt great satisfaction about know the humanity of myself better."——**做决定的目的之一，是更了解自己。**

**[他说]** 来自 CEO 系列的决策观：
- 2012-02-03 *What is CEO's Job* [一手]，引 Ben Horowitz："CEO needs to make decisions with **very limited information, always**! The expectation to understand more about it is just not realistic. That actually gave me **great relief**." —— 他**接受信息永远不足**，并因此释然。
- 2012-07-18 *CEO's Responsibility Framework* [一手]："strategy, priority, and people"；"To get rid of ambiguity is the key."——决策的核心是**消除模糊**。
- 2011-10-25 *Try to be a Good CEO* [一手]：创业的三条学习——**focus、cost、surround yourself with the right people**。

**[二手]** 网易科技《十年奔跑，远离喧嚣》：
- 2011 年 58/赶集打广告大战，他**主动不参战**，理由是参战就得堆销售、变重、不可持续——结果 58 长到 1.5 万人，百姓网保持 158 人。
- 他的词："出离心"——"远离喧嚣的世界，你一定会失去很多的机会……但可以获得内心的安稳。"
- 他承认从北京回来要"缓很长时间"，因为北京的人觉得他这套是"傻"。

## 6. 言行一致 vs 不一致 —— 汇总

## 7. 对"写作 perspective"技能的提炼要点

1. **决策被还原成"选择"，不是"意志力"或"命运"**。他反复说 "It is all about the decision you make about what is important." 写他时，遇到"坚持/转型"的话题，应落到"这是一个可以反复重做的选择"。

2. **大决定写得冷静、像做攻略；小决定反而写成方法论长文**。金额越大越平静，越小越纠结——而且他**把这种非理性当研究对象**，不掩饰。

3. **当下的职业叙事 = 叙事润滑**。重大转身的当下，他倾向用比喻把决定说得"自然、不痛、合理"（成熟的果实）。真实评价要等情绪沉淀。写作时可保留这种"当下合理化 / 事后修正"的双层。

4. **人生事件 → 认知框架**。婚姻写成"对称的巧合"，当爸爸写成"长期规划+镜子"，故乡写成"地理+历史"。他几乎不写纯情感，总把私人经历转译成一个思考工具。

5. **接受信息不足、接受自己的不完美**，并从中获得"relief"。"CEO makes decisions with very limited information, always" → "great relief"。

6. **博客=思考工具/冥想**，这是十年不变的内核。媒介（英文博客→微博→公众号→西语博客→视频创业）一直在换，"用写作/记录来逼自己思考、并帮到别人"这个目的从未变。

7. **公开辩论是默认动作**。被质疑就公开长文回应（eBay 文章 7240 词回应、回应阮一峰）。"in a typical blogger manner, I response publicly."
